A little about the developers of City Life.

This is an app for web developers; if you’re looking for a change, our app can help. Find the type of city that fits your needs! As upcoming programmers, job security is an important issue. Secondary to that is where you live! Any beginner programmer doesn’t need to stress about how much rent is, how much a cup of coffee costs (and let’s admit it, we all need our cups of joe), and how fast our internet is. What we’ve accomplished is two-fold: We’ve built an app that finds relevant job data, and linked it to data on the cities and the quality of life therein.
